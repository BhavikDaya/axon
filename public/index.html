AI Limitations

AI outputs may be inaccurate, incomplete, or fabricated. This tool uses a large language model that can and does make clinical errors. Every output must be reviewed and verified by a qualified clinician before any use.

Clinician responsibility. You remain fully professionally and legally responsible for any documentation bearing your name, regardless of how it was drafted.

Privacy & Legal (South Africa)

This is a public demo. Do not enter real patient data. Health information constitutes special personal information under POPIA Section 26. Processing it on a public platform without lawful basis, a data processing agreement, and appropriate safeguards is unlawful.
Data transits third-party servers. Input text is sent to Anthropic's API for processing and is subject to Anthropic's privacy policy. No data is stored by Axon, but Anthropic's infrastructure is not POPIA-certified for South African health data.
HPCSA. Use of this tool for real patient documentation does not transfer your professional obligations. Patient confidentiality under HPCSA guidelines applies regardless of the tools used.
Demo only. This deployment is a technology demonstration using a fictional patient transcript. For research into clinical AI documentation tools, contact the developer.
